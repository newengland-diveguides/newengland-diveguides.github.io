Additional Safety

    Safety Considerations and Tips
  1. Before heading out to your dive, be a part of the planning and brief all members to ensure everyone is on the same page
  2. Equip yourself properly, your equipment is your lifeline underwater
  3. Remember to always BREATH and monitor the air supply
  4. Bring an O2 and first aid kit equipped with AED
  5. Stay within your limits and never dive if you are feeling physically or mentally unfit
